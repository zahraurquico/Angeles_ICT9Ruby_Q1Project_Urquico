O.B. Montessori Center

Welcome to the O.B. Montessori Center in the Philippines!

  • O.B. Montessori Center is a pre-kindergarten to Grade 12 co-educational institution that provides a strong educational foundation as an alternative to international schools.
  • The school aims to develop learners who are confident, responsible, and capable of adapting to the demands of a constantly changing society.
  • Through meaningful programs and a supportive learning environment, OBMC encourages students to grow academically, socially, and personally.

At Operation Brotherhood Montessori Center, children are guided through a learner-centered approach inspired by the principles of Dr. Maria Montessori, promoting independence, discipline, and lifelong learning.
